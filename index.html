Rich Dad poor Dad

Robert T. Kiyosaki.

Rich Dad Poor Dad explores the financial lessons Kiyosaki learned from his two "dads": his biological father (the "poor dad"), who valued job security and traditional education, and his best friend's father (the "rich dad"), who focused on entrepreneurship and wealth-building. The book emphasizes the importance of financial literacy, investing in assets, and thinking differently about money. It challenges conventional beliefs about work, savings, and debt, advocating for a mindset shift to achieve financial independence.
